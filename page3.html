World,
 Dropout Studios was founded a little over a year ago. Without any clear path or plan, I was able to develop whatever I wanted and mostly took on ideas and projects as they presented themselves. From today on, I will be moving ahead full speed formulating a platform that allows creatives, artists, consumers, and everyone in between, to thrive and grow. I want to deliver a full-fledged system by the summer of 2019. It will be beyond any other projects I have created to date, and hopefully will grow and build until it is beyond me. In its current state; Dropout Studios has 5 artists signed, a concert coming in March that is already selling tickets, 2 clothing lines, a graphic design firm, video production company, and so on. I want to introduce a blog run by the artists and creators I support, a magazine that features overlooked content creators, monthly or quarterly concerts, and more. There are thousands of talented artists that create amazing content and are not acknowledged. My goal is provide all of the resources these people need to develop and thrive.
Until next time,
Gabe Rosenfeld
